About Us

"We are a team of professionals consisting of Corporate Lawyers, Litigating Solicitors, Trademark Attorneys, Patent Agents, Finance Professionals, Company Secretaries, GST Practitioners and Chartered Accountants. The partners of the firm carry experience of more than 10 years in Legal and Secretarial arena. The firm works passionately and provides ethical services to the clients by combining the knowledge of Company Secretaries and Lawyers. Furthermore, the experts associated with the firm have strategic experience and are brilliant minds from the top universities of the world.

We provide a comprehensive range of services and solutions in the field of Accountancy, Companies Compliance, Corporate Advisory, Intellectual Property Rights, Secretarial and Taxation. We are a team comprising of prominent Management and Legal Consultants who provide aid and advice on the matters pertaining to the Law, Administration and Finance inclusive of Litigation, Statutory Records, Book-keeping, and Consultation. The firm has affiliations and tie-ups on an international level which blends the firm to work together successfully and address the issues according to different territorial jurisdictions. Our strength lies in the efforts of our teammates, who are truly the asset of the organization. We as a team are committed to serve you and we assure you to provide help at the time of duress."
